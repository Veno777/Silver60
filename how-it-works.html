How Silver60 Works

Our AI-powered security service protects you from online scammers using simple, easy-to-understand technology.

1

Install Silver60

Download our easy-to-use app on your computer, tablet, or smartphone. Our team can help you set it up with a simple phone call if needed.

2

Silver60 Monitors Your Online Activity

Our system quietly checks emails, websites, and messages for suspicious content. We never read your personal information - only scan for known scam patterns.

3

Get Clear Alerts About Potential Scams

When Silver60 spots something suspicious, you'll see a simple warning message explaining what might be wrong and asking if you want to proceed.

4

Ask For Help When Needed

Not sure about an alert? Click the "Help Me" button to speak with a friendly support person who can explain the situation in plain language.

5

Learn To Recognize Scams

Silver60 includes optional short lessons to help you understand and recognize common scams on your own.

What Silver60 Protects You From

Email Scams

Phishing emails that try to steal your personal information or fake emails pretending to be from family members asking for money.

Fake Websites

Websites that look legitimate but are designed to steal your credit card information or login details.

Phone Scams

Alerts about known scam phone numbers and guidance about suspicious callers claiming to be from government agencies or tech support.
